Introducing The HUUK
Moment NFT Social Platform

Huuk is a platform where content creators can earn a fair share of the revenue generated by their own work.

This is Huuk

Create, Share and Sell your moments using Huuk App with AI, AR/VR Capability.

We are a decentralized platform in Web 3.0 using IPFS. Users have complete control over their videos and access their contents using NFT as a key for the encrypted videos.

How to earn

Easy Peasy NFTs

Cut, edit & convert videos from other platforms from the comfort of your phone. Let Huuk help turn your content into real money.

One Click To Other NFT Marketplaces

Publish and sell your moments to other large marketplace such as OpenSea for more potential of making money.

Multiple Ways To Earn

Create-2-Earn - Create content and earn NFT-2-Earn - Convert video to NFT to earn Watch-2-Earn - Watch videos Engage-2-Earn - Like and comment on videos
